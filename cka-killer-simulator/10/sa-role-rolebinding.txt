Create SA, Role and Role binding in NameSpace project-hamster

01. Create Role Binding:
    $ kubectl -n project-hamster create sa processor 

02. Create Role:
    $ kubectl -n project-hamster create role processor --verb=create --resource=secrets --resource=configmap 

03. Create Role Binding:
    $ kubectl  -n project-hamster create rolebinding --role=processor --serviceaccount project-master:processor
